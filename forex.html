India's Top Cashback Credit Cards

Cashback credit cards offer a percentage of the spending back as cash rewards. It essentially means savings on every rupee spent. Apart from cashback, credit cards often come with additional benefits like discounts on partner merchants, travel benefits, lounge access, and others, which can further enhance savings.

Forex

Forex markup is an additional charge applied by banks on international transactions made with credit cards. It's a percentage of the transaction amount charged over the base exchange rate. Lower markup means better savings on foreign currency charges. Cards with 0% markup offer the best value for international spending.

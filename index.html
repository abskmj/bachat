1% Cashback on paying Credit Card Bills

1% cashback upto ₹750 per month on paying Credit Card bills using selected HDFC Bank debit cards

  • Open HDFC Bank mobile application or internet banking website
  • Navigate to 'Bill Pay > Add Biller > Credit Card' to add a biller
  • Enter and submit details of the card to register it as a biller
  • Select the biller for the payment
  • Enter the amount to be paid
  • Select debit card as the payment method
  • Make the payment
  • Cashback is credited as Reward Points in a week
  • Navigate to 'Cards > Debit Cards -> Redeem Reward Points' to redeem them
  • Redeemed Reward Points are credited in HDFC Savings account in a week

  • Payments using HDFC EasyShop Platinum and Millennia debit Cards
  • Cashback upto ₹750 on HDFC EasyShop Platinum debit card
  • Cashback upto ₹400 on HDFC Millennia debit card
  • Not applicable for HDFC Credit Card bill payments
  • Not applicable on payments using HDFC Savings account
  • Not applicable on payments using Payzapp mobile application
